TOPIC 1.3.1: CLOSE PROTECTION TEAM STRUCTURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Team Configurations:

SOLO OPERATOR:
- Single CPO
- All responsibilities
- Low-profile work
- Executive protection common
- Resource limited
- Must be highly versatile

TWO-PERSON TEAM:
- Most common commercial
- Team Leader + Operative
- Basic coverage
- Driver/PPO combination
- Limited but effective

THREE TO FOUR PERSON TEAM:
- Better coverage
- Allows rotation
- SAP capability
- Vehicle options
- Common for celebrities

LARGE TEAMS (5+):
- High-profile principals
- 24/7 coverage
- Multiple roles filled
- Advance party possible
- Shift rotation
- Diplomatic/VIP

Key Roles in Team:

TEAM LEADER (TL):
- Overall command
- Decision maker
- Principal liaison
- Operational planning
- Team coordination
- Responsible for success/failure

PERSONAL ESCORT SECTION (PES):
- Immediate protection
- Close to principal
- Walking formations
- Vehicle movements
- First response

SECURITY ADVANCE PARTY (SAP):
- Reconnaissance
- Venue security
- Route checking
- Liaison with others
- Prepares for arrival

RESIDENTIAL SECURITY TEAM (RST):
- Home protection
- Access control
- Monitoring systems
- Static duties
- Handover to mobile team

SECURITY DRIVER:
- Vehicle operation
- Defensive driving
- Evasive driving
- Route knowledge
- Vehicle preparation

TOPIC 1.3.3: PERSONAL ESCORT SECTION (PES) ROLES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Purpose of PES:
- Immediate close protection
- Closest to principal
- First line of defence
- Evacuation responsibility
- Constant proximity

PES Positions:

PERSONAL PROTECTION OFFICER (PPO):
- Immediately beside principal
- Often called "body man/woman"
- Closest physical protection
- Evacuation lead
- Most trusted position

REAR COVER:
- Behind principal
- Watches rear threats
- Secondary evacuation
- Supports PPO

FLANKERS:
- Either side
- Crowd management
- Lateral threat coverage
- Maintain formation

POINT:
- In front
- Identifies threats ahead
- Clears path
- Liaison with SAP

PES Responsibilities:
- Maintain formation
- 360-degree awareness
- Threat identification
- Immediate response
- Principal evacuation
- Cover and shield
- Communication

Key PES Skills:
- Observation
- Fitness
- Formation discipline
- Communication
- Quick reactions
- Physical intervention

TOPIC 1.3.4: SECURITY ADVANCE PARTY (SAP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SAP Purpose:
- Prepare venues before arrival
- Check routes
- Identify risks
- Coordinate with venue security
- Enable smooth visit

SAP Tasks:

VENUE RECONNAISSANCE:
- Layout understanding
- Entry/exit points
- Emergency exits
- Holding rooms
- Safe areas
- Hazard identification

LIAISON:
- Venue management
- Venue security
- Police if needed
- Event organisers
- Other stakeholders

ROUTE CHECKING:
- Primary route clear
- Alternate routes identified
- Choke points noted
- Safe havens identified
- Timing confirmed

SECURITY PREPARATION:
- Sterile areas established
- Search if required
- Access control setup
- Communication check
- Team positioning

REPORTING:
- Report to TL
- Update intelligence
- Confirm arrangements
- Identify changes

SAP Timing:
- Arrive before principal
- Allow adequate time
- Complete checks fully
- Ready before arrival
- Handover to PES

What does SAP stand for in close protection operations?

C) Surveillance and Protection
D) Standard Action Plan

coordinating with venue security, and preparing for the principal's visit. 
They arrive before the main team to ensure everything is secure.
