Question 1 [Easy]
What is the correct priority sequence immediately after physical 
intervention?

A) Document, Communicate, Secure, Medical
B) Secure, Medical, Communicate, Preserve
C) Medical, Document, Preserve, Communicate
D) Communicate, Preserve, Medical, Secure

Correct Answer: B

Explanation: The correct priority sequence after physical intervention 
is: SECURE (ensure scene is safe and threat neutralised), MEDICAL (check 
for injuries, first aid, call ambulance if needed), COMMUNICATE (contact 
emergency services and supervisor), PRESERVE (protect evidence, identify 
witnesses). Scene security must come first before any other actions.
