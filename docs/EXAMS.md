## Overview

This document contains accurate exam structure and metadata for all four SIA (Security Industry Authority) qualifications. This data is used throughout the application for:

- Exam selection and routing
- Mock exam generation
- Progress tracking
- Analytics and reporting

**Regulatory Body:** Security Industry Authority (SIA) - UK Government

**Awarding Bodies:** Highfield, HABC, City & Guilds, and other SIA-approved organizations

## Door Supervisor (DS)

### Qualification Details

**Full Name:** Level 2 Award for Door Supervisors in the Private Security Industry

**Career Outcomes:**
- Door Supervisor (Bouncer)
- Event Security
- Retail Security
- Corporate Reception Security
- Festival/Concert Security

**Special Note:** Door Supervisor licence holders can also work as Security Guards without obtaining a separate licence.

### Exam Format

**Total MCQ Exams:** 2

| Exam | Units Covered | Questions | Duration | Pass Mark |
|------|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Exam 1 | Units 1 & 3 (combined) | 40 | 60 min | 70% (28/40) |
| Exam 2 | Unit 2 | 20 | 30 min | 70% (14/20) |

## Close Protection (CP)

### Qualification Details

**Full Name:** Level 3 Certificate for Working as a Close Protection Operative within the Private Security Industry

**Career Outcomes:**
- Close Protection Operative (Bodyguard)
- Executive Protection Specialist
- VIP Security
- Residential Security Team (RST)
- Maritime Security (with additional qualifications)
- Hostile Environment Work (with additional training)

**Special Notes:**
- Close Protection licence holders can also work as Door Supervisors without obtaining a separate licence
- **Prerequisite Required:** MUST hold Level 3 First Aid at Work (FAW) or FREC3 BEFORE starting training

### Exam Format

**Total MCQ Exams:** 4

| Exam | Units Covered | Questions | Duration | Pass Mark |
|------|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Exam 1 | Unit 1 (Principles of Working as a CPO) | 52 | 80 min | 70% (37/52) |
| Exam 2 | Unit 2 (Working as a CPO) | 30 | 45 min | 70% (21/30) |
| Exam 3 | Unit 3 (Conflict Management for CP) | 20 | 30 min | 70% (14/20) |
| Exam 4 | Unit 7 (Physical Intervention Skills) | 30 | 45 min | **80% (24/30)** |
| **Total** | | **132** | **200 min** | |

**Additional Assessments:**
- **Unit 4:** Portfolio of evidence (Terrorism Threat Awareness)
- **Unit 5:** Portfolio of evidence (Door Supervision for CPOs)
- **Unit 6:** Practical assessment (CP Physical Intervention)

**Note:** Unit 7 has an elevated 80% pass mark due to critical safety content (errors can be fatal).
