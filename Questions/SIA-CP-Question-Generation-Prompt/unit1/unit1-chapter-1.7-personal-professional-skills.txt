THE QUALIFICATION

THE EXAM

- 52 multiple choice questions

WHY THIS CHAPTER MATTERS
------------------------
Close protection is a service industry where relationships, communication,
and professionalism are as important as physical skills. CPOs work closely
with high-profile individuals and must demonstrate impeccable personal and
professional standards. Soft skills often determine career success.

Question 1 [Easy]
A CPO's duty of confidentiality regarding principal information continues...

A) Until the assignment ends
B) Until the contract expires
C) For five years after employment
D) Indefinitely

Correct Answer: D

Explanation: Confidentiality is a lifelong obligation. A CPO must never 
disclose information about principals, even after the assignment or 
employment has ended. This includes not writing tell-all books, giving 
media interviews, or discussing clients with anyone. Professional 
reputation depends on this.
