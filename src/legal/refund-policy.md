## 1. Your Rights Under UK Law

### 1.1 Consumer Rights Act 2015

Under the **Consumer Rights Act 2015**, you have the right to:

- A **14-day cooling-off period** from the date of purchase
- A refund for digital content that is faulty, not as described, or not fit for purpose
- Clear information about your cancellation rights before purchase

### 1.2 Digital Content Specific Rights

As our service provides digital content (online educational materials), the following applies:

- You have 14 days from purchase to request a refund
- **However**, if you begin accessing the content (e.g., start using the platform, attempt questions), you acknowledge that you are requesting immediate access and waiving your automatic right to a full refund
- We implement a fair use policy (detailed below) to balance consumer rights with protection against abuse

1. **Acknowledgment:** We will confirm receipt within 24 hours (business days)

If your refund is approved:

- **Account Access:** Your account will be downgraded to free tier (if applicable) or closed

### 3.5 Refund Timeline

| Step | Timeframe |
|------|-----------|
| **Acknowledgment** | Within 24 hours |
| **Review & Decision** | 1-2 business days |
| **Processing (if approved)** | 5-7 business days |
| **Bank/Card Processing** | 3-10 business days (varies by bank) |

### 3.6 Confirmation

Once the refund is processed:

## 4. Non-Refundable Circumstances

### 4.1 After 14 Days

Refund requests submitted **more than 14 days after purchase** are not eligible for a refund, except in cases of:

- Technical issues preventing access to content (which we were unable to resolve)
- Billing errors (duplicate charges, incorrect amount)
- Faulty or misdescribed content (subject to investigation)

### 4.2 Excessive Usage

Refunds will be **denied** if you have:

- Completed **20% or more** of the available questions
- Taken **3 or more** complete mock exams
- Accessed the platform extensively for more than 10 hours

**Rationale:** Excessive usage indicates you have received substantial value from the service.

### 4.3 Policy Violations

Refunds will be **denied** if your account has:

- Violated our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy
- Been suspended or terminated for misconduct
- Engaged in fraudulent activity, including:
  - Chargeback abuse
  - Multiple refund requests using different accounts
  - Sharing account credentials
  - Attempting to scrape or download content

### 4.4 Exam Failure

We **cannot** offer refunds based on:

- Failure to pass the official SIA exam
- Dissatisfaction with exam results
- Personal study outcomes

**Important:** Our platform provides exam preparation materials. Exam success depends on many factors including your study habits, prior knowledge, and the official exam format. We do not guarantee exam success.

### 4.5 Change of Mind After Excessive Use

If you have used more than 20% of the content, your "change of mind" is not grounds for a refund under our policy or UK consumer law (as you have explicitly requested and consumed digital content).
