## 3. Prohibited Uses

The following activities are **STRICTLY PROHIBITED** and will result in immediate account suspension or termination:

### 3.1 Unauthorized Access and Security Violations

❌ **Account Sharing:**
- Sharing your login credentials with others
- Allowing multiple people to use your account
- Logging in from multiple devices simultaneously (except for your own devices)

❌ **Unauthorized Access:**
- Attempting to access other users' accounts
- Bypassing authentication or security measures
- Exploiting security vulnerabilities (please report them instead)
- Using stolen or unauthorized payment methods

❌ **Hacking and Intrusion:**
- Attempting to gain unauthorized access to our systems, servers, or databases
- Probing, scanning, or testing vulnerabilities without permission
- Breaching or circumventing security or authentication measures

---

### 3.2 Content Scraping and Intellectual Property Violations

❌ **Scraping and Data Extraction:**
- Using automated tools (bots, scrapers, crawlers) to extract our content
- Systematically downloading or copying questions and answers
- Using screen capture software to mass-download content
- Extracting data through the API or any other automated means

❌ **Content Reproduction:**
- Copying our questions, explanations, or materials for distribution
- Reproducing our content on other websites or platforms
- Creating derivative works based on our proprietary content
- Reverse engineering our question database or algorithms

❌ **Distribution and Resale:**
- Selling, reselling, or commercially exploiting our content
- Distributing our materials to non-users
- Sharing screenshots, PDFs, or copies of our questions publicly
- Creating competing services using our content

---

### 3.3 Fraudulent and Illegal Activities

❌ **Payment Fraud:**
- Using stolen credit cards or payment information
- Initiating fraudulent chargebacks
- Creating multiple accounts to exploit promotions or refund policies
- Attempting to gain free or unauthorized access to paid content

❌ **Identity Fraud:**
- Providing false or misleading registration information
- Impersonating another person or entity
- Creating fake accounts or using false identities
- Misrepresenting your affiliation with any organization

❌ **Illegal Activities:**
- Using the Platform for any unlawful purpose
- Violating any UK or international laws
- Facilitating illegal activities (e.g., selling SIA licenses fraudulently)
- Money laundering or other financial crimes

---

### 3.4 Abuse and Misconduct

❌ **Harassment and Abuse:**
- Harassing, threatening, or abusing other users
- Harassing or abusing our support staff or employees
- Using offensive, discriminatory, or hateful language
- Engaging in cyberbullying or intimidation

❌ **Spam and Unsolicited Communications:**
- Sending spam or unsolicited messages through the Platform (if messaging features exist)
- Using the Platform to promote other products or services
- Posting or transmitting unsolicited advertising or promotional materials

❌ **Disruptive Behavior:**
- Intentionally disrupting the Platform or other users' experience
- Flooding our servers with requests (DoS/DDoS attacks)
- Submitting false reports or abuse complaints
- Engaging in any behavior that harms the Platform's integrity

---

### 3.5 Exam Cheating and Academic Dishonesty

❌ **Exam Misconduct:**
- Using our practice questions to cheat on the actual SIA exam (e.g., memorizing answers without understanding)
- Sharing exam answers with others during an official SIA exam
- Misrepresenting our Platform as official SIA material
- Using our content to assist others in cheating

**Note:** While our Platform is designed for exam preparation, you must still take the official SIA exam honestly and in accordance with SIA rules.

---

### 3.6 Malicious Activities

❌ **Malware and Harmful Code:**
- Uploading or transmitting viruses, malware, or harmful code
- Introducing worms, trojans, or other malicious software
- Attempting to corrupt or damage our systems or data

❌ **System Interference:**
- Interfering with or disrupting our servers, networks, or services
- Imposing an unreasonable load on our infrastructure
- Attempting to crash, overload, or impair the Platform

---

### 3.7 Misuse of Content

❌ **Misrepresentation:**
- Claiming our content is official SIA material
- Misrepresenting the source or authorship of our content
- Suggesting affiliation with or endorsement by the SIA

❌ **Misinformation:**
- Intentionally providing false information to other users
- Spreading misinformation about the SIA exam or licensing process
- Misrepresenting the effectiveness or guarantees of our Platform

## 4. Reporting Violations

### 4.1 How to Report

If you become aware of any violation of this Acceptable Use Policy:

**Email:** abuse@siaexamprep.co.uk
**Subject:** "AUP Violation Report"

**Include:**
- Description of the violation
- Evidence (screenshots, links, etc.)
- Date and time of the incident
- Any relevant account information (if known)

### 4.2 Our Investigation

We will:

- Review all reports within 2-5 business days
- Investigate the alleged violation
- Take appropriate action based on our findings
- Notify you of the outcome (if appropriate)

### 4.3 False Reports

Submitting false or malicious reports is itself a violation of this policy and may result in account termination.

## 5. Consequences of Violations

### 5.1 Warning

**First-time minor violations** may result in:

- A written warning via email
- Temporary account restriction
- Requirement to acknowledge this policy

### 5.2 Temporary Suspension

**Repeated or moderate violations** may result in:

- Account suspension for 7-30 days
- Loss of access to the Platform during suspension
- Review before reinstatement

### 5.3 Permanent Termination

**Serious or repeated violations** will result in:

- Immediate and permanent account termination
- No refund of any payments made
- Ban from creating new accounts (IP and email block)
- Possible legal action (see Section 5.4)

**Examples of serious violations:**
- Hacking or unauthorized access attempts
- Scraping or mass downloading content
- Payment fraud or chargebacks abuse
- Harassment or threats
- Distribution of our content for commercial purposes

### 5.4 Legal Action

In cases of serious violations, we may:

- Report illegal activities to law enforcement
- Pursue civil legal action for damages
- Seek injunctive relief to prevent further violations
- Cooperate with authorities in criminal investigations

### 5.5 No Refunds for Policy Violations

**Important:** If your account is suspended or terminated for violating this policy, you are NOT entitled to a refund, regardless of when the violation occurred or how much content you have accessed.
